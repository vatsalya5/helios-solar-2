Who We Are

Building Reliable, Utility-Scale Clean Energy Infrastructure

Helios Solar is an independent renewable energy infrastructure company focused on the development and operation of utility-scale solar power assets. We partner with utilities, industrial consumers, landowners, and investors to deliver reliable, long-term clean energy solutions.

Our team combines technical expertise, financial discipline, and regulatory experience to execute projects from early-stage development through commissioning and long-term operations.

Our Business Model

Our Independent Power Producer Model

As an Independent Power Producer, Helios Solar invests in and develops renewable energy projects that generate stable, long-term cash flows through contracted power sales.

Revenue visibility is achieved through long-term power purchase agreements with utilities and corporate consumers, while operational performance is optimized through active asset management.

Why Utility-Scale Solar

Utility-scale solar has become a core component of modern power systems due to its declining cost, scalability, and compatibility with energy storage and grid modernization.
